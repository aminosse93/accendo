Our Guiding Purpose

To fuel a world where every person with a disability has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission

To provide strategic funding and support to innovative non-profit organizations that empower individuals with disabilities, fostering inclusion, independence, and a higher quality of life.

Our Vision

We envision a society that fully embraces disability as a part of human diversity, where barriers to access and opportunity are eliminated, and every individual can ignite their full potential.

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We champion self-determination and provide tools for independence.

Inclusion

We believe in a world where everyone belongs and has a voice.

Innovation

We fund creative and effective solutions to long-standing challenges.
