Our Story

Founded with a vision to create meaningful change for disability communities.

Our Foundation

Accendo Charity was established in 2020 by Kelly Woo with a clear mission: to provide strategic financial support to non-profit organizations dedicated to improving the lives of people with physical and mental disabilities.

Our Approach

We believe in the power of collaboration and strategic funding. By supporting established organizations with proven track records, we maximize our impact and ensure that every dollar creates meaningful change.
